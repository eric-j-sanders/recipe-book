CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES

Ingredients:
- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions:
1. Mix butter and sugar
2. Add eggs and vanilla
3. Mix in flour
4. Fold in chocolate chips
5. Bake at 350°F for 12 minutes
6. Do a dance with them!
7. Alex wants to do the macarena with them.
8. Alex wants to do the tango with them
